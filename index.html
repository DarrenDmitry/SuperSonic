The Process

How the SSC Works

01

Nominations Open

At the end of each RLCS season, the SSC committee selects the top performing players from every region as official nominees.

02

Community Votes

Fans worldwide cast their votes over a 14-day window. Every account gets one vote — no bots, no duplicates, just the community's voice.

03

The Cup is Awarded

The winner is announced live at the SSC ceremony. Their name is engraved on the SuperSonic Cup and entered into the Hall of Fame forever.
